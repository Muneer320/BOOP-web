# BOOP 3.0 — Brand System

---

## Mission

To make professional puzzle book creation accessible to everyone — from classroom teachers to self-publishers — by combining powerful automation with beautiful, intuitive design.

## Vision

A world where anyone can create, publish, and share puzzle books that look like they came from a professional publisher — in minutes, not hours.

---

## Brand Personality

If BOOP were a person:

| Trait | How It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Confident** | Doesn't over-explain. Lets the work speak. |
| **Creative** | Finds joy in puzzles, words, and play. |
| **Helpful** | Anticipates needs, guides without hand-holding. |
| **Craftsperson** | Cares deeply about output quality. |
| **Friendly** | Approachable, not corporate. |
| **Smart** | Powerful under the hood, simple on the surface. |

**Archetype:** The Creator + The Magician
We help people make things, and we make the process feel magical.

## Brand Elements

### Logo System

**Primary Logo:** "BOOP" in a custom wordmark (bold, geometric sans-serif with a subtle puzzle-piece detail in the "O")
**Secondary Logo:** BOOP icon (stylized "B" or puzzle-piece mark)
**Tertiary:** BOOP wordmark + tagline lockup

**Logo Usage:**
- Full lockup on landing page, marketing materials
- Icon-only on favicon, app icon, social avatars
- Wordmark-only in header (when space is constrained)

### Color Palette

See DESIGN_SYSTEM.md for full spec. Brand colors remain consistent.

### Typography

See DESIGN_SYSTEM.md for full spec.

### Imagery Style

- **Screenshots:** Clean, focused, with device frames
- **Output mockups:** Professional book spreads, not just flat PDFs
- **People:** Warm, diverse, focused on creating/sharing
- **Backgrounds:** Gradient meshes, soft geometric patterns, light textures
- **Avoid:** Stock photos of "business people pointing at whiteboards," puzzle clip art

## Brand Values

1. **Quality Above All** — Every pixel, every interaction, every output should feel considered.
2. **Delight in the Details** — The extra care is what makes BOOP memorable.
3. **Power Through Simplicity** — Complex technology, simple interface.
4. **Creator-First** — Every decision starts with "does this help the creator?"
5. **Open & Honest** — Open source, transparent about capabilities, clear about limitations.
